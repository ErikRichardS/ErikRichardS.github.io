Snake

It's classic snake.
Snake is simple. Guide the snake towards the fruit. Each fruit eaten gives 1 point and makes the snake longer. If the snake runs into any wall or itself its Game Over.
You can use the large arrow buttons to change direction.
Additionally, you can also use the W A S D keys to change direction.
