Snake

It's classic snake.
Snake is simple. Guide the snake towards the fruit. Each fruit eaten gives 1 point and makes the snake longer. If the snake runs into any wall or itself its Game Over.
Use slider to adjust update speed.
Game can be paused and resumed with the button.
Clicking on the field will turn cell from alive to dead, or dead to alive.
