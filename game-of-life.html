Game of Life

A pointless game with no defined end point.
Game of Life plays out on a 2D grid of cells. A cell can be alive or dead. If an alive cell has two or three neighbors that are also alive, it will remain alive. If a dead cell has three living neighbors, it comes alive. In all other cases a cell either becomes or remains dead.
Use slider to adjust update speed.
Game can be paused and resumed with the button.
Clicking on the field will turn cell from alive to dead, or dead to alive.
